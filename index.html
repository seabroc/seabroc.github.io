About Me

My name is Cameron Seabrook and I am majoring in Computational Science. Coming into high school I was very interested into Business Management but growing up I enjoyed setting up any electronics in my household. As time went by my parents asked if I considered majoring into something that involves computers.

For those who are upcoming student who are majoring in Computational Science it takes these steps to understand the logistics of programming

  • Every chance you get, you need to attend every class period to update your knowledge.
  • In those classes you need to follow along with the instructor to get a better understanding on what you need to do, to be a great programmer.
  • After class or during your free time, if there is any trouble you can ask for help and follow some of the book examples.

Your skills will start to improve during you time taking the course, getting yourself familiar with the materials to program on your own.
